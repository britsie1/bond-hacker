# BondHacker

**BondHacker** is a privacy-first, on-device South African Home Loan (Bond) calculator and optimization tool. It helps you visualize and execute powerful financial strategies to pay off your bond faster and save hundreds of thousands of Rands in interest.

🌐 **Try it out:** [https://bondhacker.netlify.app](https://bondhacker.netlify.app)

## Why BondHacker?

Most bond calculators only show you what happens if you pay a little extra each month. BondHacker models real-world, highly effective South African bond strategies:

- **Pay Costs in Cash (New Purchases):** See the profound 20-year impact of paying transfer duty and attorney fees out of pocket instead of capitalizing them into your loan.
- **Access Bond Hack:** See how depositing your full salary into your access bond and treating it as your main transactional account acts as a 100% tax-free investment.
- **Early Debit Order (The Timing Hack):** Move your payment date to the day after you get paid to minimize the daily interest balance.
- **Rate Renegotiation:** Visualize how asking your bank for a 0.5% rate reduction changes your trajectory.
- **Fixed Installment & Monthly Boost:** Lock in your payments or commit to a monthly extra amount.
- **Annual Escalation:** Grow your extra payments with inflation / annual salary increases.
- **Strategic Lump Sums:** Model the impact of a 13th cheque or SARS refund once-off or annually.

## Features

- **🔒 100% Private & On-Device:** No databases, no servers. All financial calculations happen directly in your browser.
- **📈 Interactive Visualizations:** Compare multiple strategies stacked against your baseline in real-time.
- **🔗 Shareable Calculations:** Safely encode and share your exact scenario with a partner or financial advisor without needing an account.
- **📱 Mobile-First Design:** Fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile browsers.
- **🌙 Dark/Light Mode:** Seamlessly adapts to your system preferences.

## Running Locally

To run BondHacker on your local machine:

1. **Clone the repository:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/britsie1/bond-hacker.git
   cd bond-hacker
   ```

2. **Install dependencies:**
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```

3. **Start the development server:**
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```

4. **Build for production:**
   ```bash
   npm run build
   ```
